Firefly Dance in Twilight Forest

A slow cinematic fly-through of a quiet forest at twilight. Fireflies float like embers, the trees sway gently in a golden mist, and in the distance, a faint waterfall glows in the fading light. Ambient Hans Zimmer–style music paints the atmosphere with deep strings and soft piano chords.
